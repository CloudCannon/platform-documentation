CloudCannon keeps a record of your billing history in the form of invoices. Each invoice contains line items for your base plan charge, any add-on purchases (such as extra users, bandwidth, custom domains, or build time), credits from plan changes, and any discounts applied to your account. You can add your billing details to invoices and set your invoices to email automatically.

## Access your invoices

You can access a complete list of all your invoices for your <comp.GlossaryTerm term="/user/glossary/o/organization.yml">Organization</comp.GlossaryTerm> by navigating to *Organization Settings* > *Invoices*.

Each invoice in the list shows its status:

* **Paid** — The invoice has been successfully charged. Click *View more* to open the full invoice.
* **Pending** — The invoice has not yet been charged. This may be your upcoming invoice for the current billing period.

You can download a PDF file for each paid invoice by clicking *View more* to open the hosted invoice page.

## What invoices contain

Your invoices include the following line items:

* Your base plan charge (e.g., "Standard plan" or "Team plan")
* Any add-on charges (e.g., "Extra users", "Extra hosting bandwidth", "Custom domains", "Extra build time")
* Credits from plan changes (prorated adjustments when you upgrade or downgrade mid-billing period)
* Discounts from any coupons applied to your account
* Account credit, if applicable

If you are on an annual *Subscription*, add-on overages are billed monthly and appear separately from your annual base plan charge.

## Configure your invoices

### Add billing details to invoices

Add your billing details, such as your physical address, to your invoices by updating your *Billing Contacts*. These details display at the top of your invoices. CloudCannon will not mail copies to your physical address.

### Set up automatic invoice emails

By default, invoices are not automatically sent to any email address. You can configure automatic invoice emails by adding an email to your *Billing Contacts* in your *Subscription* settings. Once set, you will see confirmation of the email address and the next expected invoice date in your *Billing* section.

### Update your *Billing Contacts*

1. Navigate to *Organization Settings* > *Subscription*.
2. Under the *Billing* section, click *Update Contacts*.
3. Enter your preferred email address in the top field.
4. Add your billing details (name, address) in the bottom field.
5. Click *Update Billing Contacts* to save.

CloudCannon will update your billing contact information and display it on future invoices.
