<comp.Notice info_type="permissions">
  <p><strong>Permissions required</strong></p>
  <p>Members of the <em>Owners</em> or <em>Billing</em> <a href="/documentation/user-articles/what-are-default-permission-groups/">Default Permission Groups</a>, or <a href="/documentation/user-articles/what-are-custom-permission-groups/">Custom Permission Groups</a> with the <code>org:billing:write</code> permission, can manage payment methods.</p>
</comp.Notice>

CloudCannon accepts Visa and Mastercard credit cards for payment on all paid plans. You can add, update, and manage your payment methods from the *Subscription* page.

<comp.DocShot docshot_key="CloudCannon-Documentation-Org-Settings-Subscription-Add-Credit-Card-Modal" alt="A screenshot of the plan selection page shows the available CloudCannon plans with pricing and feature details." title="The plan selection page" type="screenshot"/>

To add a credit card:

1. Navigate to *Organization Settings* by clicking on your <comp.GlossaryTerm term="/user/glossary/o/organization.yml">Organization</comp.GlossaryTerm> name in the *App Sidebar*.
2. Click *Subscription* in the settings menu.
3. Click *Add Credit Card* in the *Billing* section.
4. Enter your card details in the payment form, including the name on the card, card number, expiry date, and CVC.
5. Expand the *Additional info* section and enter your billing address (optional).
6. Click *Add Credit Card* to save your card.

CloudCannon will verify your card and add it to your *Organization*. If this is your first card, CloudCannon will set it as your default payment method.

<comp.Notice info_type="info">
  Enterprise *Organizations* may use invoicing instead of credit cards. If your *Organization* is on an Enterprise plan, your billing is managed by the CloudCannon team. Please contact our <a href="/support/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">support team</a> for Enterprise payment inquiries.
</comp.Notice>

## What happens when a payment fails

If your credit card is declined, CloudCannon retries the payment multiple times over the following weeks. For full details on the retry process and what happens to your *Sites*, please read the documentation on [how CloudCannon handles failed payments](/documentation/user-articles/how-cloudcannon-handles-failed-payments/).
