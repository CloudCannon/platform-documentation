<comp.Notice info_type="permissions">
  <p><strong>Permissions required</strong></p>
  <p>Members of the <em>Owners</em> or <em>Billing</em> <a href="/documentation/user-articles/what-are-default-permission-groups/">Default Permission Groups</a>, or <a href="/documentation/user-articles/what-are-custom-permission-groups/">Custom Permission Groups</a> with the <code>org:billing:write</code> permission, can manage payment methods.</p>
</comp.Notice>

CloudCannon accepts credit card payments on all paid plans. You can add, update, and manage your payment methods from the *Subscription* page.

If your credit card is declined, CloudCannon retries the payment multiple times over the following weeks. For full details on the retry process and what happens to your *Sites*, please read the documentation on [how CloudCannon handles failed payments](/documentation/user-articles/how-cloudcannon-handles-failed-payments/).

<comp.Notice info_type="info">
  Enterprise *Organizations* may use invoicing instead of credit cards. If your *Organization* is on an Enterprise plan, your billing is managed by the CloudCannon team. Please contact our <a href="/support/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">support team</a> for Enterprise payment inquiries.
</comp.Notice>

## Add your first credit card

To add your first credit card:

1. Navigate to the *Subscription* page under *Org Settings* by clicking on the *Org Settings* button in the *App Sidebar*.
2. Click the *Add Credit Card* button under *Billing Details*. CloudCannon will open the *Add your Credit Card* modal.
3. Enter your Credit Card details. Please double check they are correct.
4. (Optional.) Enter *Additional information*, if it is required by your bank to process payments.
5. Click the *Add Credit Card* button

CloudCannon will verify your card and automatically use this card for payments on your *Organization* plan.

## Add additional credit cards

To add additional credit cards:

1. Navigate to the *Subscription* page under *Org Settings* by clicking on the *Org Settings* button in the *App Sidebar*.
2. Click the *Change Credit Card* button under *Billing Details*. CloudCannon will open the *Manage Credit Cards* modal.
3. Click the *+ Add Credit Card* button. CloudCannon will open the *Add your Credit Card* modal.
3. Enter your Credit Card details. Please double check they are correct.
4. (Optional.) Enter *Additional information*, if it is required by your bank to process payments.
5. Click the *Add Credit Card* button

CloudCannon will verify your card and automatically use this card for payments on your *Organization* plan.

## Switch your in use credit card

To switch your in use credit card:

1. Navigate to the *Subscription* page under *Org Settings* by clicking on the *Org Settings* button in the *App Sidebar*.
2. Click the *Change Credit Card* button under *Billing Details*. CloudCannon will open the *Manage Credit Cards* modal.
3. Click the *Use Card* button next to the credit card you want CloudCannon to charge.

CloudCannon will use this card for payments on your *Organization* plan.
