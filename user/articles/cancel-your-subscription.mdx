You can cancel your paid CloudCannon *Subscription* at any time by downgrading to the free Personal plan. Cancellation does not delete your <comp.GlossaryTerm term="/user/glossary/o/organization.yml">Organization</comp.GlossaryTerm>, *Sites*, or files — it reduces your access to features and resource limits.

## Downgrade to the Personal plan

1. Navigate to *Organization Settings* by clicking on your *Organization* name in the *App Sidebar*.
2. Click *Subscription* in the settings menu.
3. Click *Review your billing plan* to open the plan selection page.
4. Click *Select Personal* at the bottom of the plan selection page.
5. If your current usage exceeds the Personal plan's limits, CloudCannon will open the *Confirm resources downgrade* modal. Review which resources exceed the new limits and follow the provided links to resolve them (e.g., removing team members or custom domains).
6. Click *Confirm downgrade*.

CloudCannon will downgrade your *Organization* to the Personal plan.

## What happens when you cancel

When you downgrade to the Personal plan:

* Your *Sites* and files remain intact, but features available only on paid plans will no longer be accessible.
* Resource limits are reduced to Personal plan levels (limited build time, bandwidth, users, and custom domains).
* If your usage exceeds the Personal plan's limits at the time of cancellation, you may need to remove resources before the downgrade takes effect.

## Cancellation vs. *Subscription* pause

Cancellation (downgrading to Personal) is a voluntary action you take from the plan selection page. A *Subscription* pause is different — it happens automatically when CloudCannon cannot collect payment after multiple retry attempts.

When your *Subscription* is paused due to failed payments:

* You cannot edit your *Sites* through CloudCannon.
* Your hosted websites are unavailable.
* You can create and download a backup of your *Site* files.

For more information on failed payments and subscription pauses, please read the documentation on [how CloudCannon handles failed payments](/documentation/user-articles/how-cloudcannon-handles-failed-payments/).

## Resume a paused *Subscription*

If your *Subscription* has been paused due to a failed payment, you can resume it by updating your payment method:

1. Navigate to *Organization Settings* > *Subscription*.
2. Click the *Review payment* link in the payment warning banner.
3. Add or update your credit card details.
4. Follow the prompts to resume your *Subscription*.

CloudCannon will reactivate your *Organization* and restore access to your *Sites*.

For more information on managing your payment methods, please read the documentation on [managing your payment methods](/documentation/user-articles/manage-your-payment-methods/).
