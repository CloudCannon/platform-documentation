<comp.Notice info_type="permissions">
  <p><strong>Permissions required</strong></p>
  <p>Members of the <em>Owners</em> or <em>Billing</em> <a href="/documentation/user-articles/what-are-default-permission-groups/">Default Permission Groups</a>, or <a href="/documentation/user-articles/what-are-custom-permission-groups/">Custom Permission Groups</a> with the <code>org:billing:write</code> permission, can add a coupon to a subscription.</p>
</comp.Notice>

If you have a promotional coupon, you can apply it to your <comp.GlossaryTerm term="/user/glossary/s/subscription.yml">Subscription</comp.GlossaryTerm> for a discount on your billing plan. Coupons are applied from the *Your Subscription Plan* page and the discount appears on your billing summary once applied.

<comp.Notice info_type="info">
  Each *Organization* can only have one active coupon at a time. Applying a new coupon will replace any existing coupon on your *Subscription*.
</comp.Notice>

To add a coupon to your *Subscription*:

1. Navigate to the *Subscription* page under *Org Settings* by clicking on the *Org Settings* button in the *App Sidebar*.
2. Click the *Review your billing plan* link in the billing summary. CloudCannon will navigate you to the *Your Subscription Plan* page.
3. Click the *Add a coupon* button in the billing summary. CloudCannon will open the *Add Coupon* modal.
4. Enter your coupon code in the *Coupon ID* field.
5. Click the *Apply Coupon* button.

<comp.DocShot docshot_key="CloudCannon-Documentation-Org-Settings-Subscription-Add-Coupon-Modal" alt="A screenshot of the Add Coupon modal with a Coupon ID input field and an Apply Coupon button." title="The Add Coupon modal" type="screenshot"/>

CloudCannon will validate your coupon and apply the discount to your *Subscription*. The discount will appear as a line item in your billing summary, showing the coupon name and the discount amount (e.g., "20% off forever").

If your coupon code is not valid, CloudCannon will display an error message. Double check that you have entered the coupon code correctly and try again. If the issue persists, please contact our <a href="/support/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">support team</a>.

<comp.Notice info_type="info">
  Enterprise *Organizations* have custom billing arrangements. If you are on an Enterprise plan, please contact our <a href="/support/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">support team</a> for coupon inquiries.
</comp.Notice>
