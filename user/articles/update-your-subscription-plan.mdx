<comp.Notice info_type="permissions">
  <p><strong>Permissions required</strong></p>
  <p>Members of the <em>Owners</em> or <em>Billing</em> <a href="/documentation/user-articles/what-are-default-permission-groups/">Default Permission Groups</a>, or <a href="/documentation/user-articles/what-are-custom-permission-groups/">Custom Permission Groups</a> with the <code>org:billing:write</code> permission, can change the subscription plan.</p>
</comp.Notice>

You can upgrade or downgrade your <comp.GlossaryTerm term="/user/glossary/s/subscription.yml">Subscription</comp.GlossaryTerm> at any time.

When you change plans mid-billing period, CloudCannon calculates a prorated adjustment and applies a credit to your new plan for the unused time remaining on your previous plan. These adjustments appear as line items on your next invoice. Proration applies when you upgrade, downgrade, or switch between monthly and annual billing so you won't be double-charged when switching plans.

## Upgrade your plan

To upgrade your CloudCannon *Subscription*:

1. Navigate to the *Subscription* page under *Org Settings* by clicking on the *Org Settings* button in the *App Sidebar*.
2. Click the *Review your billing plan* link in the billing summary. CloudCannon will navigate you to the *Your Subscription Plan* page.
3. Click the *Change your plan* button. CloudCannon will navigate you to the *Update your plan* page.
4. Select whether you want to be billed monthly or annually using the *Billing Period* toggle.
5. Select your desired plan (e.g., Standard or Team) using the *Select* buttons. CloudCannon will navigate you to the *Your Subscription Plan* page.
6. Review the *Plan resources* and *Estimated bill*.
7. Click the *Complete checkout* button.

CloudCannon will apply your new plan immediately.

## Downgrade your plan

To downgrade your CloudCannon *Subscription*:

1. Navigate to the *Subscription* page under *Org Settings* by clicking on the *Org Settings* button in the *App Sidebar*.
2. Click the *Review your billing plan* link in the billing summary. CloudCannon will navigate you to the *Your Subscription Plan* page.
3. Click the *Change your plan* button. CloudCannon will navigate you to the *Update your plan* page.
4. Select whether you want to be billed monthly or annually using the *Billing Period* toggle.
5. Select your desired plan using the *Select* buttons. CloudCannon will navigate you to the *Your Subscription Plan* page.
6. If your current usage exceeds the target plan's limits (users, bandwidth, custom domains, or build time), CloudCannon will open the *Confirm resources downgrade* modal. This modal shows which resources exceed the new plan's limits and provides links to resolve them (e.g., removing team members or custom domains). Review the resource changes and click *Confirm downgrade*.
7. Review the *Plan resources* and *Estimated bill*.
8. Click the *Complete checkout* button.

CloudCannon will apply your new plan immediately.

<comp.Notice info_type="important">
  Downgrading may reduce your access to features and resource limits included in your current plan. Review the *Confirm resources downgrade* modal carefully before proceeding.
</comp.Notice>

## Legacy plans

If your *Organization* is on a legacy plan (a plan that is no longer available for new subscriptions), you can continue using it. However, if you change to a different plan, you cannot return to your legacy plan.

<comp.Notice info_type="important">
  Changing away from a legacy plan is a one-way action. Make sure the new plan meets your needs before confirming the change.
</comp.Notice>

## Enterprise plans

Enterprise *Organizations* have custom billing arrangements managed by the CloudCannon team. Self-serve plan changes are not available for Enterprise plans. To change your Enterprise plan, please contact our <a href="/support/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">support team</a>.
