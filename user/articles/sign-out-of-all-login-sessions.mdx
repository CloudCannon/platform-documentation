## Sign out of all sessions

The *Logout of all sessions* button signs you out of every active session across all your devices.

<comp.Notice info_type="important">
  If your current session is included, CloudCannon will redirect you to the login page after signing out.
</comp.Notice>

To sign out of all sessions:

1. Navigate to the *Sessions* page under *Account Settings*.
2. Click the *Logout of all sessions* button.
3. Read the confirmation message and click *Confirm* to proceed.

CloudCannon will end all active sessions. If your current session was included, you will be redirected to the login page.
