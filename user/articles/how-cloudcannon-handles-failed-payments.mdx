Credit cards sometimes decline for unexpected reasons. When a payment fails, CloudCannon gives you time to resolve the issue before taking any action on your *Subscription*.

After a failed payment, CloudCannon retries the charge four times over the next three weeks. During this period, your *Subscription* remains active and your *Sites* continue to function normally.

If all retry attempts are unsuccessful, CloudCannon will pause your *Subscription*.

## Payment warning states

When a payment issue occurs, CloudCannon displays a warning banner on your *Subscription* page. The banner describes the issue and provides a *Review payment* link to help you resolve it. You may encounter the following payment states:

* **Card action required** — Your payment requires additional verification, such as 3D Secure authentication. Click *Review payment* to complete the verification step in your browser.
* **Card failed** — Your credit card was declined. Click *Review payment* to update your payment method.
* **Subscription paused** — Your *Subscription* has been paused because CloudCannon could not collect payment after several retry attempts. Click *Review payment* to update your payment method and settle the outstanding balance. You will also see this banner if your [Free Trial](/documentation/user-articles/what-is-cloudcannons-free-trial/) has ended without a payment method on file.

## 3D Secure authentication

Some card payments require 3D Secure (3DS) verification — an additional authentication step required by your bank. When this happens, CloudCannon displays the *Card action required* banner on your *Subscription* page. Click *Review payment* to complete the 3DS verification in your browser.

## What happens when your *Subscription* is paused

When your *Subscription* is paused due to failed payments:

* You will not be able to edit your *Sites* through CloudCannon.
* Your hosted websites will be unavailable.
* You can create and download a backup of your *Site* files.
* Other pages in CloudCannon will display a "Billing issue" message directing you to resolve the payment problem.

<comp.DocShot docshot_key="CloudCannon-Documentation-Org-Settings-Subscription-Unpaid-Warning" alt="A screenshot of the Subscription page with a warning banner indicating the subscription has been paused due to a failed payment." title="The Subscription page with a paused subscription warning" type="screenshot"/>

To resume your *Subscription* after a payment failure:

1. Navigate to the *Subscription* page under *Org Settings* by clicking on the *Org Settings* button in the *App Sidebar*.
2. Click the *Review payment* link in the payment warning banner.
3. Add a new credit card or update your existing payment method and follow the prompts to complete the outstanding payment.

CloudCannon will reactivate your <comp.GlossaryTerm term="/user/glossary/o/organization.yml">Organization</comp.GlossaryTerm> and restore access to your *Sites* and hosted websites.

For more information on managing your payment methods, please read the documentation on [managing your payment methods](/documentation/user-articles/manage-your-payment-methods/). For any issues with billing, please reach out to our <a href="/support/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">support team</a>.
