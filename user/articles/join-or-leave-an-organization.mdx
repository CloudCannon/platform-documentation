## Join an Organization

To join an [Organization](/documentation/user-articles/what-is-an-organization/) in CloudCannon, [an existing member must invite you](/documentation/user-articles/add-a-team-member-to-your-organization/). When someone invites you, CloudCannon will send you an email invitation with the name of the *Organization*.

When you accept the invitation, CloudCannon will add you to the *Organization* as a <comp.GlossaryTerm term="/user/glossary/t/team-member.yml">Team Member</comp.GlossaryTerm>, automatically assigning you to the [Permission Groups](/documentation/user-articles/what-are-permission-groups/) selected by the person who invited you. You can also decline an invitation, but a current member of the *Organization* must re-invite you if you want to join at a later date.

How you accept your invitation will depend on whether you already have a CloudCannon account.

### If you don't have a CloudCannon account yet

To create your account and accept an invitation:

1. In your email invitation, click the *Sign up for CloudCannon* button. CloudCannon will open in your internet browser.
2. Complete the account creation setup. CloudCannon will open the *Organizations* page.
3. Click the *Accept* button next to the *Organization* you want to join.

CloudCannon shows any *Organization* that has invited you under the *You've been invited* heading on the *Organizations* page.

If you don't recognise any of the invitations, you can click the *Create organization* button to start your own *Organization* instead.

<comp.DocShot docshot_key="CloudCannon-Documentation-Organizations-Page-Empty-With-Invitations" alt="A screenshot of the You've been invited page shows a pending invitation with Accept and Decline buttons, and an option to create your own Organization." title="The You've been invited page" type="screenshot"/>

### If you already have a CloudCannon account

When you have pending invitations, CloudCannon shows a number badge on your *Avatar* in the bottom left of the <comp.GlossaryTerm term="/user/glossary/a/app-sidebar.yml">App Sidebar</comp.GlossaryTerm>.

<comp.DocShot docshot_key="CloudCannon-Documentation-App-Sidebar-User-Pending-Invite" alt="A screenshot of the user element in the App Sidebar shows a number badge on the avatar." title="The user element with a pending invitation badge" type="ui-snippet"/>

To accept an invitation from another *Organization* while you're logged in:

1. Click on your *Avatar* in the *App Sidebar* to open the *Account Menu*.
2. Select *Pending invitations* to open the *Pending invitations* modal.
3. Click the *Accept* button next to the *Organization* you want to join.

<comp.DocShot docshot_key="CloudCannon-Documentation-App-Sidebar-Account-Menu-Pending-Invite" alt="A screenshot of the open Account Menu shows the Pending invitations option at the top." title="The Account Menu with a pending invitation" type="ui-snippet"/>

<comp.DocShot docshot_key="CloudCannon-Documentation-Organization-Invites-Modal" alt="A screenshot of the Pending invitations modal shows one invitation with Accept and Decline buttons." title="The Pending invitations modal" type="screenshot"/>

If you are a member of multiple *Organizations*, you can also view and accept pending invitations from the <comp.GlossaryTerm term="/user/glossary/o/organizations-browser.yml">Organizations Browser</comp.GlossaryTerm>, which you can open by selecting *Organizations* in the *Account Menu*.

## Leave an Organization

<comp.Notice info_type="important">
  If you are the only <comp.GlossaryTerm term="/user/glossary/o/owners.yml">Owner</comp.GlossaryTerm> of an *Organization*, you cannot leave until you add another Team Member to the *Owners* *Permission Group*.
</comp.Notice>

To leave an *Organization* in CloudCannon:

1. Navigate to the *My Permissions* page under *Org Settings*.
2. Click the *Leave Organization* button in the top right.
3. Click the *Leave Organization* button in the confirmation modal to confirm.

<comp.DocShot docshot_key="CloudCannon-Documentation-Org-Settings-My-Permissions-Page" alt="A screenshot of the My Permissions page shows one Permission Group and the Leave Organization button." title="The My Permissions page" type="screenshot"/>

CloudCannon will remove you from this *Organization*.
