## Join an Organization

To join an [Organization](/documentation/user-articles/what-is-an-organization/) in CloudCannon, [an existing member must invite you](/documentation/user-articles/add-a-team-member-to-your-organization/). When someone invites you, CloudCannon will send you an email invitation with the name of the Organization. How you accept will depend on whether you already have a CloudCannon account.

### If you don't have a CloudCannon account yet

The most common way you might join an *Organization* is by invitation via email, before you have a CloudCannon account.

To join an *Organization* via account creation:

1. Open your email invitation from an existing *Organization* member and click the link to create an account. CloudCannon will open in your Internet browser.
2. Follow the account creation steps. CloudCannon will open the x page.
3. Click the *Accept* button...

CloudCannon will add you as a *Team Member* of that *Organization*, in the [Permission Groups](/documentation/user-articles/what-are-permission-groups/) selected by the person who invited you.

If you don't recognize the *Organization* invitations on the x page, you can [create your own Organization]().

### If you already have a CloudCannon account

If you are already using CloudCannon when someone invites you to another Organization:

1. Log in to CloudCannon using the link in your email invitation.
2. Click on your *Avatar* in the bottom left of the *App Sidebar* to open the *Account Menu*. When you have pending invitations, CloudCannon shows a number badge on your *Avatar* and a *Pending invitations* option at the top of the *Account Menu*.
3. Select *Pending invitations* to open the *Pending invitations* modal, then click the *Accept* button next to the Organization you want to join.

<comp.DocShot docshot_key="CloudCannon-Documentation-Organization-Invites-Modal" alt="A screenshot of the Pending invitations modal shows one invitation with Accept and Decline buttons." title="The Pending invitations modal" type="screenshot"/>

If you are a member of multiple Organizations, you can also view and accept pending invitations from the *Organizations Browser*, which you can open by selecting *Organizations* in the *Account Menu*.

### After you respond to an invitation

Once you accept an invitation, CloudCannon will add you to the Organization. You will automatically be a member of the [Permission Group(s)](/documentation/user-articles/what-are-permission-groups/) selected by the person who invited you.

Alternatively, you can decline an invitation by clicking the *Decline* button. However, once you decline an invitation to an Organization, a current member of that Organization must re-invite you if you want to join at a later date.

## Leave an Organization

To leave an Organization in CloudCannon:

1. Navigate to the *My permissions* page under *Org settings.*
2. Click the *Leave Organization* button in the top right.
3. Confirm that you want to leave this Organization by double clicking the *Leave Organization* button in the confirmation modal.

CloudCannon will remove you from this Organization.
