<comp.Notice info_type="permissions">
  <p><strong>Permissions required</strong></p>
  <p>Members of the <em>Owners</em> or <em>Billing</em> <a href="/documentation/user-articles/what-are-default-permission-groups/">Default Permission Groups</a>, or <a href="/documentation/user-articles/what-are-custom-permission-groups/">Custom Permission Groups</a> with the <code>org:billing:write</code> permission, can update Billing Contacts.</p>
</comp.Notice>

You can add billing contact information to the header of your invoices, and specify an email address to which CloudCannon will forward invoices. By default, your invoices do not have any billing contact information on them.

<comp.Notice info_type="important">
  <p>CloudCannon does not physically mail invoices, even if you have an address in your billing contact information.</p>
</comp.Notice>

<comp.DocShot docshot_key="CloudCannon-Documentation-Org-Settings-Billing-Contact-Information-Modal" title="The Billing Contacts modal" alt="A screenshot of the Billing Contacts modal in CloudCannon, showing fields for billing name, email, and address." type="screenshot" />

To add billing contact information:

1. Navigate to the *Subscription* page under *Org Settings* by clicking on the *Org Settings* button in the *App Sidebar*.
2. Click the *Update contacts* button under *Billing Details*. CloudCannon will open the *Billing Contacts* modal.
3. Enter any contact information you want to appear on your invoice. If you enter an email address, CloudCannon will email your invoices to that address.
4. Click the *Update contact information* button.


CloudCannon will update the *Billing Details* section on the *Subscription* page to show which email address invoices are sent to and when the next invoice is due.
