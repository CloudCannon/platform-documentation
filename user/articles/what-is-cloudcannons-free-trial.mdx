CloudCannon offers a 21-day <comp.GlossaryTerm term="/user/glossary/f/free-trial.yml">Free Trial</comp.GlossaryTerm> of the Standard plan for new <comp.GlossaryTerm term="/user/glossary/o/organization.yml">Organizations</comp.GlossaryTerm>. The trial gives your *Organization* full access to Standard plan features without requiring a payment method.

During the trial, your *Organization* has access to all Standard plan features, including:

* Additional users and *Client Sharing*
* Custom domains
* Increased bandwidth and build time limits
* All editing and collaboration features included in the Standard plan

## Eligibility

The free trial is available once per *Organization*. How the trial starts depends on how the *Organization* is created:

* **New accounts** — When you [create a CloudCannon account](/documentation/user-articles/create-a-cloudcannon-account/) for the first time, CloudCannon automatically creates your first *Organization* and starts the trial immediately. No extra steps are needed.
* **Existing accounts** — If you already have a CloudCannon account and [create a new Organization](/documentation/user-articles/create-a-new-organization/), the new *Organization* starts on the Personal plan. When you try to use a feature that requires the Standard plan, you may be able to start a new trial.

For more information on creating an *Organization*, please read the documentation on [creating a new Organization](/documentation/user-articles/create-a-new-organization/).

You can track how many days remain in your trial using the countdown in the *App Sidebar*.

## What happens when the trial ends

Before the trial ends, you can add a payment method to continue on a paid plan. If you stay on the Standard plan, your trial continues and billing begins automatically when the trial period is over. If you choose a different plan (e.g., Team), the trial ends immediately and billing begins right away.

If you do not add a payment method before the trial ends, your *Organization* downgrades to the Personal plan. Your *Sites* and files remain intact, but features and resource limits are reduced to Personal plan levels.

For more information on adding a payment method, please read the documentation on [managing your payment methods](/documentation/user-articles/manage-your-payment-methods/). For a step-by-step walkthrough of starting a trial and setting up billing, see the [Manage your CloudCannon Subscription](/documentation/user-guides/manage-your-cloudcannon-subscription/) guide.

## Extending your trial

If you need more time to evaluate CloudCannon, you can extend your trial. If you are a member of CloudCannon's <a href="/partner-program/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Partner Program</a>, you receive unlimited trial periods for your client *Organizations*. Otherwise, you can <a href="/support/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">contact our support team</a> to request a trial extension if you need additional time.
