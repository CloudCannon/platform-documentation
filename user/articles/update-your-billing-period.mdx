<comp.Notice info_type="permissions">
  <p><strong>Permissions required</strong></p>
  <p>Members of the <em>Owners</em> or <em>Billing</em> <a href="/documentation/user-articles/what-are-default-permission-groups/">Default Permission Groups</a>, or <a href="/documentation/user-articles/what-are-custom-permission-groups/">Custom Permission Groups</a> with the <code>org:billing:write</code> permission, can change the billing period.</p>
</comp.Notice>

Paid <comp.GlossaryTerm term="/user/glossary/s/subscription.yml">Subscription</comp.GlossaryTerm> plans in CloudCannon are available on a monthly or annual billing cycle:

* **Monthly** — Your card is charged automatically at the start of each month for the upcoming month. Monthly billing offers flexibility if you want to change plans frequently.
* **Annual** — Your card is charged automatically once a year for the upcoming year at a discounted rate compared to monthly billing.

You can switch between billing periods at any time.

When you change billing period mid-billing period, CloudCannon calculates a prorated adjustment and applies a credit to your new plan for the unused time remaining on your previous plan. These adjustments appear as line items on your next invoice. Proration applies when you upgrade, downgrade, or switch between monthly and annual billing so you won't be double-charged when switching plans.

To update your billing period:

1. Navigate to the *Subscription* page under *Org Settings* by clicking on the *Org Settings* button in the *App Sidebar*.
2. Click *Subscription* in the settings menu.
3. Click the *Review your billing plan* button in the billing summary.
4. Select whether you want to be billed monthly or annually using the *Billing Period* toggle.
5. Select your current plan (e.g., Lite, Standard, or Team) using the *Select* buttons. CloudCannon will navigate you to the *Your Subscription Plan* page.
6. Review the *Plan resources* and *Estimated bill*.
7. Click the *Complete checkout* button.

CloudCannon will prorate the change on your next invoice.
