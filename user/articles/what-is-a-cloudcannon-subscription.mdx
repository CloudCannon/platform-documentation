A <comp.GlossaryTerm term="/user/glossary/s/subscription.yml">Subscription</comp.GlossaryTerm> is a billing plan associated with your <comp.GlossaryTerm term="/user/glossary/o/organization.yml">Organization</comp.GlossaryTerm>. Your *Subscription* determines which features are available on CloudCannon, how many resources your *Organization* can use, and how you are billed.

<comp.Notice info_type="info">
  For the most up-to-date information on CloudCannon plan pricing and features, please see the [pricing page](/pricing/).
</comp.Notice>

Each *Organization* in CloudCannon has its own *Subscription*. Members of the *Owners* or *Billing* [Default Permission Groups](/documentation/user-articles/what-are-default-permission-groups/) can view and manage the *Subscription* through *Organization Settings*. If you use [Custom Permission Groups](/documentation/user-articles/what-are-custom-permission-groups/), you can grant billing access with the `org:billing:read` and `org:billing:write` permissions.

All billing settings are available under *Organization Settings* in the *App Sidebar*. From there, you can access the *Subscription* page to view your billing summary, manage payment methods, and review your plan.

## Plan tiers

CloudCannon offers several plan tiers to suit different team sizes and needs:

* **Personal** — A free plan for individuals. Includes limited build time, bandwidth, and one user.
* **Standard** — For small teams who need more resources, collaboration features, and custom domains.
* **Team** — For larger teams with higher resource limits, more users, and additional hosting bandwidth.
* **Enterprise** — For organizations with custom requirements. Enterprise plans include dedicated support, custom billing arrangements, and advanced security features such as mandatory SSO.

<comp.DocShot docshot_key="CloudCannon-Documentation-Update-Your-Plan-Monthly-Comparison" alt="A screenshot of the plan selection page shows the available CloudCannon plans with pricing and feature details." title="The plan selection page" type="screenshot"/>

Each plan includes a set amount of users, bandwidth, custom domains, and build time. If you need more than your plan includes, you can purchase <comp.GlossaryTerm term="/user/glossary/a/add-on.yml">add-ons</comp.GlossaryTerm> to extend your limits. For more information, please read the documentation on [add-ons](/documentation/user-articles/what-are-add-ons/).

<comp.Notice info_type="info">
  New to CloudCannon? The [Manage your CloudCannon Subscription](/documentation/user-guides/manage-your-cloudcannon-subscription/) guide walks you through setting up billing from scratch.
</comp.Notice>

## Billing periods

Paid plans are available on a monthly or annual billing cycle. Annual subscriptions offer a discount compared to monthly billing. You can switch between billing periods at any time from the plan selection page.

For more information, please read the documentation on [switching your billing period](/documentation/user-articles/switch-your-billing-period/).

## Free trial

New *Organizations* on the Personal plan are eligible for a 21-day <comp.GlossaryTerm term="/user/glossary/f/free-trial.yml">Free Trial</comp.GlossaryTerm> of the Standard plan. The trial gives you full access to Standard plan features without requiring a payment method. For more information, please read the documentation on [CloudCannon's free trial](/documentation/user-articles/what-is-cloudcannons-free-trial/).

## Enterprise billing

Enterprise *Organizations* have custom billing arrangements managed directly by the CloudCannon team. If you are on an Enterprise plan, your *Subscription* page displays your plan details and a direct line to your account manager. Self-serve plan changes and credit card management are not available for Enterprise plans.

For questions about Enterprise billing, please contact our <a href="/support/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">support team</a>.
