The *Sessions* page under *Account Settings* lists all active and previous login sessions for your CloudCannon account. You can use this page to review where your account is signed in and to sign out of all sessions at once.

To navigate to your sessions:

1. Log in to CloudCannon.
2. Navigate to the *Sessions* page under *Account Settings*.

CloudCannon will display two lists: *Active sessions* and *Previous sessions*.

## Session details

Each session card shows the following information:

* **Browser and operating system** — The browser and OS detected when the session was created (e.g., Chrome on Windows).
* **IP address** — The IP address used to sign in.
* **Signed in** — How long ago the session was created.
* **Last active / Ended** — For active sessions, how long ago the session was last used. For previous sessions, when the session ended.
* **Login method** — The method used to sign in (Email, GitHub, GitLab, Bitbucket, or SAML/SSO).
* **MFA** — Shown if multi-factor authentication was used during sign in.

Your current session is highlighted with a *Current* tag.

## Search sessions

You can filter your sessions by IP address using the search field above each list.

To search your sessions:

1. Navigate to the *Sessions* page under *Account Settings*.
2. Enter an IP address in the *Search by IP address* field above either the *Active sessions* or *Previous sessions* list.

CloudCannon will filter the list to show only sessions matching that IP address.

## Sign out of all sessions

The *Logout of all sessions* button signs you out of every active session across all your devices.

<comp.Notice info_type="important">
  If your current session is included, CloudCannon will redirect you to the login page after signing out.
</comp.Notice>

To sign out of all sessions:

1. Navigate to the *Sessions* page under *Account Settings*.
2. Click the *Logout of all sessions* button.
3. Read the confirmation message and click *Confirm* to proceed.

CloudCannon will end all active sessions. If your current session was included, you will be redirected to the login page.
