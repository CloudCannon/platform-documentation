Welcome to the Manage your CloudCannon Subscription guide!

This guide is for anyone getting started with a paid CloudCannon <comp.GlossaryTerm term="/user/glossary/s/subscription.yml">Subscription</comp.GlossaryTerm> for the first time. Whether you're an <comp.GlossaryTerm term="/user/glossary/o/organization.yml">Organization</comp.GlossaryTerm> owner setting up billing for your team or a team member who has been asked to manage the *Subscription*, we'll walk you through everything step by step. No prior CloudCannon billing knowledge is assumed.

If you're already on a paid plan and need to make a specific change (like updating your credit card or switching plans), you may find our [billing articles](/documentation/user-articles/what-is-a-cloudcannon-subscription/) more useful — they cover individual tasks in detail.

## What we'll cover

In this guide, we'll walk you through:

* Choosing the right subscription plan for your team
* Adding a payment method to activate your subscription
* Configuring <comp.GlossaryTerm term="/user/glossary/a/add-on.yml">add-ons</comp.GlossaryTerm> to extend your plan's resource limits
* Understanding your billing summary and invoices

By the end, you'll have a fully configured paid subscription and know where to find all your billing information.

## Before we begin

This guide assumes you already have:

* **CloudCannon account** — If you don't have one yet, you can <a href="https://app.cloudcannon.com/register" target="_blank" rel="noopener">create a free account</a>.
* **Organization** — An *Organization* is your team's workspace in CloudCannon. Every CloudCannon account starts with one. If you need an additional *Organization*, you can [create a new one](/documentation/user-articles/create-a-new-organization/). Your *Organization* is where your *Sites*, team members, and subscription all live.
* **Billing permissions** — You need to be a member of the *Owners* or *Billing* [Default Permission Group](/documentation/user-articles/what-are-default-permission-groups/) to manage billing. If you're the person who created the *Organization*, you're already an Owner. If someone else manages your *Organization*, ask them to add you to the *Billing* group.

## A quick orientation

All billing settings in CloudCannon live under *Organization Settings*. To get there:

1. Click your *Organization* name in the *App Sidebar* (the left-hand navigation panel).
2. Click *Subscription* to view your billing summary, payment methods, and plan details.

CloudCannon offers a free Personal plan and a 21-day <comp.GlossaryTerm term="/user/glossary/f/free-trial.yml">Free Trial</comp.GlossaryTerm> of the Standard plan, so you can explore before committing to a paid subscription. For a full comparison of plans and pricing, visit the <a href="https://cloudcannon.com/pricing/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">pricing page</a>.

Let's get started! In the next step, we'll choose a subscription plan for your *Organization*.
