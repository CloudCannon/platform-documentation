<comp.Notice info_type="permissions">
  **Permissions required**

  Anyone can create a new Organization on CloudCannon. When you create a new Organization, CloudCannon will automatically add you to the Owners [Default Permission Group](/documentation/user-articles/what-are-default-permission-groups/) for that Organization.
</comp.Notice>

An <comp.GlossaryTerm term="/user/glossary/o/organization.yml">Organization</comp.GlossaryTerm> is how you bring your team, *Sites*, and resources together in one place. You can create and be a part of multiple *Organizations* in CloudCannon. There are two ways to create an *Organization*, depending on whether you are new to CloudCannon or already have an account.

## Create your first Organization

When you [create a CloudCannon account](/documentation/user-articles/create-a-cloudcannon-account/), CloudCannon automatically creates your first *Organization* for you. The *Organization* name is based on your first name (e.g., "Jane Organization"). You can rename it later in *Organization Settings*.

CloudCannon also starts a free 21-day <comp.GlossaryTerm term="/user/glossary/f/free-trial.yml">Free Trial</comp.GlossaryTerm> of the Standard plan for your new *Organization*. For more information, please read the documentation on [CloudCannon's free trial](/documentation/user-articles/what-is-cloudcannons-free-trial/).

<comp.Notice info_type="info">
  If you sign up by accepting an invitation to someone else's *Organization*, CloudCannon does not automatically create an *Organization* for you. You can create one later using the steps below.
</comp.Notice>

## Create an additional Organization

If you already have a CloudCannon account and want to create another *Organization*:

1. Click on your avatar in the bottom left of the *App Sidebar* to open the *User Menu*.
2. Select *Organizations*.
3. On the *Organizations* page, click *Create organization*.
4. Enter a name for your *Organization* in the *Organization name* field.
5. Click *Create Organization*.

CloudCannon will create your new *Organization* and redirect you to its dashboard. From there, you can configure your <comp.GlossaryTerm term="/user/glossary/s/subscription.yml">Subscription</comp.GlossaryTerm>, invite team members, and start building *Sites*.
