A <comp.GlossaryTerm term="/user/glossary/s/site-icon.yml">Site Icon</comp.GlossaryTerm> is a PNG image that CloudCannon uses to identify your *Site* across the app. When you add a `siteicon.png` file to the root of your repository, CloudCannon displays it in several places throughout the interface.

<comp.DocShot docshot_key="CloudCannon-Documentation-Site-Header-With-Site-Icon" alt="A screenshot of the Site Header shows the Site Icon displayed next to the Site name." title="The Site Header with a Site Icon" type="screenshot"/>

Your *Site Icon* appears in the following places:

* The *Site Header*, next to your *Site* name at the top of the page.
* The *Sites Browser* on your *Site* card.
* The *Project Browser* on *Site* cards within your *Project*.
* Activity feed and *Site* search menus.

<comp.DocShot docshot_key="CloudCannon-Documentation-Sites-Browser-With-Site-Icon" alt="A screenshot of the Sites Browser shows Site Icons displayed on Site cards." title="The Sites Browser with Site Icons" type="screenshot"/>

If you don't add a *Site Icon*, CloudCannon displays a default placeholder icon.

## Image recommendations

Your *Site Icon* file must meet the following requirements:

* PNG format (`.png`).
* Named exactly `siteicon.png`.
* Placed at the root of your repository (`/siteicon.png`).

CloudCannon resizes your *Site Icon* to 350x350px for display. For the best results, use a square image as non-square images may be cropped or padded., and avoid detailed photographs or images with small text, as these don't display well at smaller sizes. CloudCannon also supports images with transparent backgrounds.

<comp.Notice info_type="info">
  CloudCannon does not use your *Site Icon* as the browser tab favicon for your website. To set a favicon, add one to your site files as part of your build process.
</comp.Notice>

For instructions on adding a *Site Icon* to your *Site*, please read our documentation on [Adding a Site Icon](/documentation/developer-articles/add-a-site-icon/).
