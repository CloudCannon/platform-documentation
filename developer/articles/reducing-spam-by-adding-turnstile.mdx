<a href="https://developers.cloudflare.com/turnstile/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Cloudflare Turnstile</a> embeds a CAPTCHA in your page to help prevent spam. Turnstile requires additional configuration in your Site and Inbox Settings.

### Adding <a href="https://developers.cloudflare.com/turnstile/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Turnstile</a> to an inbox

1. In your Org Settings, under *Hosting &gt; Forms* either create a new inbox or select *manage* on an existing inbox
2. Select <a href="https://developers.cloudflare.com/turnstile/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Turnstile</a> as your captcha provider.
3. Sign into Cloudflare and follow <a href="https://developers.cloudflare.com/turnstile/get-started/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">this guide</a> to get your Turnstile captcha keys.
4. In CloudCannon, enter your secret as your captcha secret key, and your site key as your captcha site key.

### Adding <a href="https://developers.cloudflare.com/turnstile/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Turnstile</a> to your site

1. Connect your site to your Turnstile enabled inbox in your Site Settings, under *Hosting &gt; Forms.*
2. Ensure that you have selected *Require Captcha* in your site inbox settings.
3. Add a form to your site and connect it to your inbox by following the install instructions in your site inbox settings.
4. Add Turnstile to that form by following the instructions in the <a href="https://developers.cloudflare.com/turnstile/get-started/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Turnstile documentation</a>

Once configured, any form submissions that fails to validate will return a 401 error page.
