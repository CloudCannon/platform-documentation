The Lite plan is a CloudCannon subscription plan available exclusively for [Client Organizations](/documentation/developer-articles/create-a-client-organization/) in the CloudCannon Partner Program. It is not available for your own Partner Organization or for standard Organizations outside the partner program.

The Lite plan is suited to clients with simpler site requirements. For a full comparison of plan features and pricing, see the [pricing page](/pricing/).

You can put a Client Organization on the Lite plan at creation time, or change an existing Client Organization to Lite at any time.

## Select Lite when creating a client organization

When you create a Client Organization, CloudCannon prompts you to choose a pricing plan before the organization is created.

To select Lite at creation time:

1. Navigate to your *Partner Dashboard*.
2. Click the *\+ New client* button.
3. On the *Create a new Client Organization* page, enter a name for the Client Organization and configure the Unified Configuration setting.
4. Under *Pricing plan*, select *Lite*.
5. Click the *Create Client Organization* button.

CloudCannon will create the Client Organization on the Lite plan.

## Change an existing client organization to the Lite plan

You can change an existing Client Organization to the Lite plan from within that organization's billing settings. You must be an Owner of the Client Organization, or have a Custom Permission Group with the `org:billing:write` permission in that organization.

To change a Client Organization to the Lite plan:

1. Open the Client Organization by clicking its name in your *Partner Dashboard* or switching to it from the *Organization* switcher.
2. Navigate to *Org Settings* by clicking *Org Settings* in the *App Sidebar*.
3. Click the *Review your billing plan* link in the billing summary. CloudCannon will navigate you to the *Your Subscription Plan* page.
4. Click the *Change your plan* button. CloudCannon will navigate you to the *Update your plan* page.
5. Select *Lite* using the *Select* button. CloudCannon will navigate you to the *Your Subscription Plan* page.
6. If the Client Organization's current usage exceeds the Lite plan's limits, CloudCannon will open the *Confirm resources downgrade* modal. Review which resources exceed the new limits and follow the provided links to resolve them.
7. Review the *Plan resources* and *Estimated bill*.
8. Click the *Complete checkout* button.

CloudCannon will apply the Lite plan immediately.

<comp.Notice info_type="info">
  The Lite plan is only available for Client Organizations managed by a Partner Organization. If the *Lite* option does not appear on the plan selection page, confirm that you are viewing a Client Organization and not your Partner Organization or a standard Organization.
</comp.Notice>
