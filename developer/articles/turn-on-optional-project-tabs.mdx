<comp.Notice info_type="permissions">
  **Permissions required**

  Members of the Owners and Developers [Default Permission Groups](/documentation/user-articles/what-are-default-permission-groups/), or [Custom Permission Groups](/documentation/developer-articles/what-are-custom-permission-groups/) with the `project:settings:details:write` permission, can show or hide the optional *Project* tabs.
</comp.Notice>

Each <comp.GlossaryTerm term="/user/glossary/p/project.yml">Project</comp.GlossaryTerm> in CloudCannon has three optional tabs in its top-level navigation: *Publishing*, *Pull Requests*, and *Branches*. You can show or hide each tab independently from the *Project Settings* details form.

Hiding a tab only removes it from the *Project* navigation. CloudCannon still runs the underlying automations — for example, hiding the *Pull Requests* tab does not turn off *Deploy Previews*. To stop those automations, please read our documentation on [Configure Deploy Previews](/documentation/developer-articles/configure-deploy-previews/).

## Open the Optional Project Tabs settings

To reach the optional tab toggles:

1. Click *Projects* in the *App Sidebar*. CloudCannon will open the *Projects Browser*.
2. Select the *Project* you want to configure. CloudCannon will open the *Project Sites Browser*.
3. Click the *Project Settings* tab.
4. Select *Details* from the list of sections.

Scroll down to the *Optional Project Tabs* fieldset.

## Show or hide the Publishing tab

The *Publishing* tab shows a graph of how the *Sites* in your *Project* publish to each other.

1. In the *Optional Project Tabs* fieldset, switch the *Publishing* toggle on or off.
2. Click the *Update project* button.

## Show or hide the Pull Requests tab

<comp.Notice info_type="pricing">
  **The *Pull Requests* tab is available on our [Team and Enterprise plans](/pricing/).**
</comp.Notice>

The *Pull Requests* tab shows a list of open and draft *Pull Requests* in the *Git Repository* connected to your *Project*.

1. In the *Optional Project Tabs* fieldset, switch the *Pull Requests* toggle on or off.
2. Click the *Update project* button.

If your subscription does not include *Deploy Previews*, the *Pull Requests* toggle is disabled and CloudCannon shows an *Upgrade to enable* link.

The *Pull Requests* tab is also gated by a dedicated read permission. Even with the toggle on, a *Team Member* who does not have permission to read pull requests on the *Project* will not see the tab.

## Show or hide the Branches tab

The *Branches* tab shows a list of branches in the *Git Repository* connected to your *Project*, and the *Sites* connected to each branch.

1. In the *Optional Project Tabs* fieldset, switch the *Branches* toggle on or off.
2. Click the *Update project* button.
