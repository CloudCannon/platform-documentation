<comp.Notice info_type="permissions">
  **Permissions required**

  Members of the *Owners* and *Developers* [Default Permission Groups](/documentation/user-articles/what-are-default-permission-groups/), or [Custom Permission Groups](/documentation/developer-articles/what-are-custom-permission-groups/) with the `project:settings:details:write` permission, can configure *Branch Defaults* for a *Project*.
</comp.Notice>

*Branch Defaults* control how CloudCannon configures new branched <comp.GlossaryTerm term="/user/glossary/s/site.yml">Sites</comp.GlossaryTerm> in your <comp.GlossaryTerm term="/user/glossary/p/project.yml">Project</comp.GlossaryTerm>. For more information, please read our documentation on [what Branch Defaults are](/documentation/developer-articles/what-are-branch-defaults/).

These instructions assume you have already [created a Project](/documentation/developer-articles/create-a-project/).

To configure the *Branch Defaults* for your *Project*:

1. Click *Projects* in the *App Sidebar*. CloudCannon will open the *Projects Browser*.
2. Select the *Project* you want to configure. CloudCannon will open the *Project Sites Browser*.
3. Click the *Project Settings* tab.
4. Select *Branch Defaults* from the *Section Navigation*.
5. Under *Publish settings*, use the radio buttons to select the *Default Publish mode* for all branched *Sites* in the *Project*.
6. (Optional.) Enable the *Delete branched site after publishing* checkbox to have CloudCannon remove branched *Sites* from the *Project* after they publish.
7. Under *Authentication settings*, use the *Default authentication method* dropdown to choose an authentication method for all branched *Sites* in the *Project*.
8. (Optional.) If you selected *Password*, enter a password in the *Default site password* field.
9. (Optional.) If your *Organization* uses [Custom Permission Groups](/documentation/developer-articles/what-are-custom-permission-groups/), enable the *Do not grant new permissions on Site branch* checkbox to stop CloudCannon adding additional permissions to *Custom Permission Groups* for branched *Sites*.
10. Click the *Update Branch Defaults* button at the bottom of the page.

CloudCannon will update your *Branch Defaults*.

<comp.DocShot docshot_key="CloudCannon-Documentation-Project-Settings-Branch-Defaults" alt="A screenshot of the Branch Defaults section in Project Settings, showing the Publish settings, Authentication settings, and Permission settings fieldsets." title="The Branch Defaults section in Project Settings" type="screenshot" />

CloudCannon applies your updated *Branch Defaults* to *Sites* created after the change. Existing *Sites* keep their current settings.
