<comp.Notice info_type="permissions">
  **Permissions required**

  Members of the Editors and Technical Editors [Default Permission Groups](/documentation/user-articles/what-are-default-permission-groups/), or [Custom Permission Groups](/documentation/developer-articles/what-are-custom-permission-groups/) with the `site:publish:write` permission, can merge a *Pull Request* from CloudCannon. Additional restrictions configured on your *Git Provider* may also apply.
</comp.Notice>

When a *Team Member* publishes from a branched <comp.GlossaryTerm term="/user/glossary/s/site.yml">Site</comp.GlossaryTerm> configured with the *Pull Request* *Publishing Method*, CloudCannon opens a <comp.GlossaryTerm term="/user/glossary/p/pull-request.yml">Pull Request</comp.GlossaryTerm> on your <comp.GlossaryTerm term="/user/glossary/g/git-provider.yml">Git Provider</comp.GlossaryTerm>. Reviewing the *Pull Request*, including viewing the diff, leaving comments, and approving, happens on your *Git Provider*, not in CloudCannon.

For more information about *Publishing Methods*, please read our documentation on [what Publishing Methods are](/documentation/user-articles/what-are-publishing-methods/).

To review a *Pull Request*, open it on your *Git Provider* (GitHub, GitLab, or Bitbucket) using your *Git Provider's* review tools. From CloudCannon, you can navigate to a *Pull Request* in two places:

* The *Pull Requests* tab on your *Project*, which lists all open *Pull Requests* in the connected <comp.GlossaryTerm term="/user/glossary/g/git-repository.yml">Git Repository</comp.GlossaryTerm>.
* The *Publishing* page of a branched *Site* with an open *Pull Request*, which shows the *Pull Request* title, description, and changes.

<comp.DocShot docshot_key="CloudCannon-Documentation-Publishing-Page-Open-Pull-Request" alt="A screenshot of the Publishing page on a Site with an open Pull Request shows the Pull Request details and buttons to Publish or Close the Pull Request." title="The Publishing page with an open Pull Request" type="screenshot" />

Once a *Pull Request* is approved on your *Git Provider*, you can merge it from your *Git Provider* or from CloudCannon. To merge from CloudCannon, click the *Publish pull request* button on the branched *Site's* *Publishing* page. CloudCannon will return an error if you do not meet any additional restrictions configured on your *Git Provider*.

If you close the *Pull Request* — either on your *Git Provider* or by clicking *Close pull request* on the *Publishing* page — the branched *Site* will retain its saved changes until a *Team Member* updates the content or deletes the *Site*.
