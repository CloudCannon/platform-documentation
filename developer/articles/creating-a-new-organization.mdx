<comp.Notice info_type="permissions">
  **Permissions required**

  Anyone can create a new Organization on CloudCannon. When you create a new Organization, CloudCannon will automatically add you to the Owners [Default Permission Group](/documentation/user-articles/what-are-default-permission-groups/) for that Org.
</comp.Notice>

An Organization is the highest level of content management on CloudCannon, containing all your websites, team members, and resources in CloudCannon. When you sign up, CloudCannon will automatically create an *Organization* for you.

You can [join multiple Organizations](/documentation/user-articles/join-or-leave-an-organization/) by invitation, or create as many Organizations as you require.

To create a new Organization:

1. Click on your *Name* or *Avatar* at the bottom of the *App Sidebar* to open the *Account Menu*.
2. Select *Create new organization*. CloudCannon will open the *Create a new organization* modal.
3. Enter a name for your Organization in the *Organization name* text field.
4. Click the *Create organization* button.

CloudCannon will forward you to the *Organization Home* page for your new Organization.

<comp.DocShot docshot_key="CloudCannon-Documentation-Create-Organization-Modal" alt="A screenshot of the Create a new organization modal shows the Organization name text field." title="The Create a new organization modal" type="screenshot"/>

<comp.Notice info_type="info">
  If you are already a member of multiple Organizations, the *Account Menu* shows an *Organizations* option in place of *Create new organization*. Select it to open the *Organizations Browser*, then click the *\+ Create organization* button on the right of your *Organization Browser Controls* to open the *Create your Organization* page.
</comp.Notice>

Once you belong to more than one Organization, you can switch between them at any time from the *Organizations Browser*, which you can open by selecting *Organizations* in the *Account Menu*. If you only belong to a single Organization, CloudCannon takes you straight into it, so the *Account Menu* shows the *Create new organization* option in place of *Organizations*.
