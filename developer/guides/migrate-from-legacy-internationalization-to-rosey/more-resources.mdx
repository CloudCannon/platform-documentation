Now that you've migrated from CloudCannon's built-in i18n to Rosey, here are some resources to help you get the most out of your setup.

## Rosey

* [Add internationalization with Rosey](/documentation/developer-guides/add-internationalization-with-rosey/) — The full Rosey setup guide, covering configuration, translation file management, and deploying a multilingual site on CloudCannon.
* [What is the Rosey CloudCannon Connector?](/documentation/developer-guides/add-internationalization-with-rosey/what-is-the-rosey-cloudcannon-connector/) — Add inline translation editing to the *Visual Editor* without requiring *Editable Regions* or Bookshop.
* <a href="https://rosey.app" target="_blank" rel="noopener">rosey.app</a> — Comprehensive Rosey documentation, including advanced configuration options.

## CloudCannon

* [Staging workflow guide](/documentation/developer-guides/staging-workflow-guide/) — Set up separate staging and production branches so Rosey runs only on your production branch, keeping the *Visual Editor* available for content editing.
