This release addressed several issues, including those affecting new payment methods, resolving *Syncing Errors*, and custom *Testing Domains* for *Client Organizations*.

## Fixes

* Fixed an issue where, in some cases, you could not add a new payment method.
* Fixed an issue where, in some cases, the *Reconnect* or *Clear and Reconnect* buttons for resolving *Syncing Errors* did not work.
* Fixed an issue preventing *Partner Organizations* from creating custom *Testing Domains* for their *Client Organizations*.
