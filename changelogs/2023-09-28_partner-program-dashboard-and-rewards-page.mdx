This release

**Features:**

* Released the Partner dashboard. This dashboard allows you to view your organization and navigate your list of clients easily.
* Released the Partner rewards page, available to members of our <a target="_blank" rel="noopener" href="https://cloudcannon.com/partner-program/">Partner Program</a>. This page lets you track your total points, how your organization earned them, and download your partner badge (Silver tier and higher). You can also view all the CloudCannon rewards your organization has earned and the rewards for the next program tier.
