Welcome to the Edit Content in CloudCannon guide!

This guide covers the basics of WYSIWYG editing on CloudCannon, including adding, removing, and duplicating files on your *Site*, editing file contents in one of four Editing Interfaces, and saving your changes.

Before we begin, this guide assumes you have a *Site* on CloudCannon with WYSIWYG and visual editing enabled. We highly recommend that you, or your developer, complete the following guides for the best CloudCannon editing experience:

* [Getting Started with Editing](/documentation/guides/getting-started-with-editing/)
* Set up Visual Editing
