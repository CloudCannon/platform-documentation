    For sites built with Eleventy, CloudCannon automatically configures two collections: Pages and Data.
    
    The Pages view lists the pages on your site. Clicking on a page will open it in an editing interface.
    
    <comp.DocsImage path="https://cc-dam.imgix.net/documentation/CloudCannon-Getting-Started-Pages-View.png" alt="A screenshot of the Pages view in the CloudCannon app shows a preview of all the pages on the example website." title="Pages view" type="screenshot"/>
    
    The Data tab shows all your data files. Clicking on a page will open it in an editing interface.
    
    <comp.DocsImage path="https://cc-dam.imgix.net/documentation/CloudCannon-Getting-Started-Data-View.png" alt="A screenshot of the Data view in the CloudCannon app shows a list of data files for the example website." title="Data view" type="screenshot"/>
