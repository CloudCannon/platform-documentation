Congratulations! If you've followed each step of this guide so far, you have set up a basic Site in CloudCannon and learnt important skills for managing your content. But, there's a lot more to CloudCannon that we haven't covered.

CloudCannon is a customizable platform, allowing you to create an experience that's right for you and your team. In the next steps of this guide, we'll cover several ways you can configure CloudCannon. You can complete these steps in any order, but we've used an order

While technically optional, we highly recommend reading through these steps to determine which parts suit your needs.

In the next steps of this guide, we'll cover how to:

* [Configure your Collections](/documentation/guides/getting-started/configure-your-collections/), such as changing the appearance of files in your Collection Browser, determining how CloudCannon adds new files to your Collection, and updating your Collection details like name and icon.
* [Configure your Editing Interfaces](/documentation/guides/getting-started/configure-your-editing-interfaces/) ... adding custom Inputs, and Structures
* [Configure your Rich Text Editors](/documentation/guides/getting-started/configure-your-rich-text-editors/), WYSIWYG toolbar, markdown engine, Snippets
* [Configure visual editing](/documentation/guides/getting-started/configure-visual-editing/) using CloudCannon Editables, allowing you to edit directly onto a preview of your webpages.
* [Configure web hosting](/documentation/guides/getting-started/configure-web-hosting/)
* [Add your team to CloudCannon](/documentation/guides/getting-started/add-your-team-to-cloudcannon/)
* [Create a Project](/documentation/guides/getting-started/create-a-project/)
* [Customize your Organization branding](/documentation/guides/getting-started/customize-your-organization-branding/)
* [Subscribe to CloudCannon](/documentation/guides/getting-started/subscribe-to-cloudcannon/), manage your billing settings, and view your invoices.
