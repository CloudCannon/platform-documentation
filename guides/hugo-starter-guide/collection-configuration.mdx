    CloudCannon provides basic content editing for Hugo without any configuration. However, we recommend you use a global configuration file to get the most out of the platform. CloudCannon gives you complete control to fine-tune the editing experience for you and your team members.
  </comp.SlotContent>
  <comp.SlotContent name="SSG_Plugin_Paragraph">
    <comp.Notice info_type="info">
      In some SSGs, such as Hugo, CloudCannon will partially define your collections for you. The CloudCannon Hugo Plugin will automatically detect the collection name, path, and output, but other inputs require configuration. You can see this information in the Configuration GUI.
    </comp.Notice>
