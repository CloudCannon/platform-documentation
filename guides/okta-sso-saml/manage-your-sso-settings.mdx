Once you have set up SSO, you can manage your Settings through the *Single Sign-On* page under *Org Settings*.

There are three tabs on the *Single Sign-On* page: *Details*, *Settings*, and *Danger Zone*.

The *Details* tab shows you all the details of your SSO configuration. These details might be important if you need to reconfigure your Identity Provider, or reference

<comp.DocShot docshot_key="CloudCannon-Documentation-Org-Settings-Single-Sign-On-Details-Tab" alt="A screenshot of the Details Tab on the Single Sign-On page shows the details of your SSO configuration." title="The Single Sign-On page" type="screenshot"/>

The *Danger Zone* tab has the *Remove SAML Authentication* button. Click this button to remove SSO from your Organization.

<comp.DocShot docshot_key="CloudCannon-Documentation-Org-Settings-Single-Sign-On-Danger-Zone-Tab" alt="A screenshot of the Danger Zone Tab on the Single Sign-On page shows the Remove SAML Authentication button." title="The Single Sign-On page" type="screenshot"/>

The majority of your SSO settings are under the *Settings* tab.

<comp.DocShot docshot_key="CloudCannon-Documentation-Org-Settings-Single-Sign-On-Settings-Tab" alt="A screenshot of the Settings Tab on the Single Sign-On page shows the Require Single Sign-On (SSO) for your Organization checkbox." title="The Single Sign-On page" type="screenshot"/>

Here, you can edit the *Initial Permission Group*, *Authn Context*, *Signing Algorithm*, and *X.509* *Certificate* details.

You can also require SSO for all team members in your Organization.
