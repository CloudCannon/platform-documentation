Permissions control what actions you can perform within your [Organization](/documentation/articles/what-is-an-organization/). Your permissions are determined by which [Permission Groups](/documentation/articles/what-are-permission-groups/) you belong to.

## Review my current Permission Groups

To view your current permissions:

1. Navigate to the *My permissions* page under *Org settings.*

<comp.Notice info_type="info">
  You can leave yourself from a Permission Group at any time. However, you may not have permission to add yourself to a Group. If you accidentally remove yourself from a Permission Group, please contact the appropriate member of your team or read our documentation on [adding team members to a Permission Group](/documentation/articles/add-a-team-member-to-a-permission-group/).
