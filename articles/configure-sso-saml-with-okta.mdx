Okta is a popular Identity Provider. To use Okta SAML with CloudCannon follow these instructions:

## Create app

Create a new SAML 2.0 application on Okta for CloudCannon.

<comp.DocsImage path="ye_olde_images/organisations-saml-1-create-app.png" alt="Screenshot of Okta SAML setup interface" title="" type="screenshot"/>

## Configure Okta

Go to CloudCannon and open *Organization Settings* / *SAML*. Copy your Issuer and Consume URL. If you do not have a SAML option in your menu contact support to get this enabled.

<comp.DocsImage path="ye_olde_images/organisations-saml.png" alt="Screenshot of SAML update interface" title="" type="screenshot"/>

Enter the Issuer and Consume URL information into Okta.

<comp.DocsImage path="ye_olde_images/organisations-saml-3-copy.png" alt="Screenshot of OKTA SAML update page" title="" type="screenshot"/>

And confirm youâ€™re using CloudCannon as an internal application.

<comp.DocsImage path="ye_olde_images/organisations-saml-4-confirm.png" alt="Screenshot of Okta SAML confirm screen" title="" type="screenshot"/>

## CloudCannon Configure

View the setup instructions for your newly created application on Okta.

<comp.DocsImage path="ye_olde_images/organisations-saml-5-view.png" alt="Screenshot of Okta SAML configuration interface" title="" type="screenshot"/>

Copy the Identity Provider Single Sign-On URL and X.509 Certificate.

Enter the Identity Provider Single Sign-On URL and X.509 Certificate information into CloudCannon.
