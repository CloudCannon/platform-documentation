To add a team member to your Site:

1. Navigate to the *Site Sharing* page under *Site settings*.
2. Click the *\+Add members* button on the right to open the *Invite team members to your Site* modal.
3. Enter the email address of the person you want to add to your Site
4. Enter the name of the team member(s) you want to add to this Site. You can also enter the email addresses of new users you want to invite to your Organization.
5. Select the Permission Group to which you want to add these team members.
6. Click the *Add member(s)* button.
