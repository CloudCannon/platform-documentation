CloudCannon’s current Beta Release is available to our Partners between December 2023 and February 2024.

To open CloudCannon Beta, click on your avatar in the bottom left of the app. In the *Account Menu*, click *Enter CloudCannon Beta*.

\[image\]

CloudCannon will reload, and a *Using CloudCannon Beta* overlay will appear in the app.

To close CloudCannon Beta, click *Exit CloudCannon Beta* in the *Account Menu*.
