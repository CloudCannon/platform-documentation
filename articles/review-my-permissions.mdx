Permissions dictate what actions you are allowed to take in your [Organization](/documentation/articles/what-is-an-organization/). Your permissions are determined by which [Permission Groups](/documentation/articles/default-permission-groups/) you belong to.

<comp.Notice info_type="info">
  You or a team member in your Organization will have permission to change which permission groups you belong to. For more information, please read our documentation on [managing your team members](/documentation/articles/managing-your-team-members/).
</comp.Notice>

To view your current permissions:

1. Navigate to the *My permissions* tab under *Org settings.*
