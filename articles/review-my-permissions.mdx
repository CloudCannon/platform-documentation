Permissions control what actions you can perform within your [Organization](/documentation/articles/what-is-an-organization/). Your permissions are determined by which [Permission Groups](/documentation/articles/what-are-permission-groups/) you belong to.

<comp.Notice info_type="info">
  You or a team member in your Organization will have permission to change which Permission Groups you belong to. For more information, please read our documentation on [adding team members to](/documentation/articles/add-a-team-member-to-a-permission-group/) or [removing team members from a Permission Group](/documentation/articles/remove-a-team-member-from-a-permission-group/).
</comp.Notice>

To view your current permissions:

1. Navigate to the *My permissions* page under *Org settings.*
