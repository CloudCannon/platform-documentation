To delete a custom [Permission Group](/documentation/articles/what-are-permission-groups/) from your [Organization](/documentation/articles/what-is-an-organization/):

1. Navigate to the *Team* page under *Org settings*.
2. Click on the *Groups* tab.
3. Under the *Groups* tab, click on the Permission Group you want to delete. This will open the *Permission Group* page.
4. Click on the *Settings* tab.
5. Click the *Delete Group* button at the bottom of the page.
6. Confirm that you want to delete this Permission Group by clicking the *Remove from Group* button in the confirmation modal.

CloudCannon will delete this Permission Group, and all members will lose any permissions associated with the Group.
