CloudCannon offers free SSL by default on every site and serves them over HTTPS. This is made possible by ZeroSSL and Cloudflare. These certificates are automatically renewed before they expire.

## Wildcard Redirect and Wildcard SSL

CloudCannon also offers wildcard redirects and a wildcard SSL Certificates for all first level subdomains of your configured custom domain.

To enable wildcard redirect and SSL on your site:

1. Go to Site Settings / Custom domain / HTTPS
2. Set *Validation Method* to *DNS TXT Record*
3. Toggle the *Generate a wildcard certificate to enable SSL* option
4. Click **Update SSL**
5. Follow the  instructions to add the TXT record to your DNS.

### Redirecting HTTP to HTTPS

To redirect all HTTP traffic to HTTPS:

1. Go to *Site Settings* / *Routing*
2. Enable the *Redirect all HTTP traffic to HTTPS* option
3. Click **Update Routing Details**
