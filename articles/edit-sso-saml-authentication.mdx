You can update the initial [Permission Group](/documentation/articles/what-are-permission-groups/) for SSO users and your X.509 Certificate at any time.

To edit your [SSO/SAML](/documentation/articles/what-is-sso-saml/) settings:

1. Navigate to the *Single Sign-On* page under *Org settings*.
2. Click on the *Settings* tab.
3. Edit your settings.
4. Click the *Update SAML Configuration* button.
