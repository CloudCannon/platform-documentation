To run your first build you must configure any command line options and build system options required for your Site. CloudCannon will remind you to do this with the *Setup* prompt in the *Site Navigation*.

<comp.Notice info_type="info">
  Building is an optional step. If your Site is dynamic, or you only want to use CloudCannon as a CMS for your Git repository, you donâ€™t need to build. For more information, please read our guide on using [CloudCannon in Headless Mode](/documentation/guides/headless-starter-guide/).
</comp.Notice>

This article assumes that you have completed the [Syncing](/documentation/articles/introduction-to-syncing/) stage and created a [CloudCannon configuration file](/documentation/articles/create-a-global-configuration-file/) for your Site, but have yet to choose between Building and [Headless Mode](/documentation/articles/what-is-headless-mode/).

<comp.DocsImage path="https://cc-dam.imgix.net/documentation/images/builds/configure-your-first-build/2024-08/CloudCannon-Documentation-Status-Page-Configure-Builds.png" alt="A screenshot of the Status page Summary tab shows the Open build configuration and I don't want to build buttons." title="The Status page Summary tab" type="screenshot"/>

To configure your first Site build:

1. Navigate to the *Summary* tab of the *Status* page by clicking the *Return to setup* button on the x in the the *Site Navigation*. Alternatively, you can click *Status* in the *Developer* section of the *Site Navigation*.
2. Click the *Open build configuration* button. CloudCannon will open your *Build configuration* page under *Site Settings*.
3. Enter your *Command line options* and *Build system options.* You can click on CloudCannon's suggested values to use them in your configuration.
4. Click the *Update Configuration and Build* button.

CloudCannon will attempt to build your Site.

<comp.Notice info_type="info">
  CloudCannon will suggest command line options for you based on your SSG and the contents of your Site files.
</comp.Notice>

<comp.DocsImage path="https://cc-dam.imgix.net/documentation/images/builds/configure-your-first-build/2024-08/CloudCannon-Documentation-Site-Settings-Build-Configuration-Setup.png" alt="A screenshot of the Build Configuration page shows blue boxes containing CloudCannon's suggestions for your command line options." title="The Build Configuration page" type="screenshot"/>

You can monitor the outcome of your first build on the *Summary* tab of the *Status* page.

<comp.DocsImage path="https://cc-dam.imgix.net/documentation/images/builds/configure-your-first-build/2024-08/CloudCannon-Documentation-Status-Page-Running-First-Build.png" alt="A screenshot of the Status page Builds tab shows the first build of a Site is running." title="The Status page" type="screenshot"/>

After a successful first build, CloudCannon will build your Site every time it syncs an update from your repository, or when you [choose to trigger a build](/documentation/articles/what-is-a-site-build/#starting-a-build). This ensures your most recent content is always live.

If your build fails for any reason, CloudCannon will suggest common solutions to troubleshoot your build. For assistance configuring your builds, please contact our friendly [support team](/support/).
