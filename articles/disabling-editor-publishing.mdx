To disable publishing, you must disconnect the publish site from the branched site.

Go to *Site Settings / Publishing* and click **Remove Publish Branch**.

<comp.DocsImage path="ye_olde_images/files-publish-publish-settings.png" alt="Publish Branch interface with branch set" type="screenshot" />

Once removed, the **Publish** tab will be removed from the branched site's settings.
