<comp.Notice info_type="permissions">
  **Permissions required**

  Members of the Owners or Developers [Default Permission Groups](/documentation/articles/what-are-default-permission-groups/), or [Custom Permission Groups](/documentation/articles/what-are-custom-permission-groups/) with the `base-domain:settings:dns` permission, can manage the DNS records for a Custom Domain.
</comp.Notice>

&nbsp;

&nbsp;

These instructions assume you have [connected a Custom Domain to your Organization](/documentation/articles/connect-a-custom-domain-to-your-organization/) and selected CloudCannon DNS as your DNS provider.

To add a DNS record to your Custom Domain:

1. Log in to CloudCannon and select the Organization with the Custom Domain to which you want to add a DNS record.
2. Click the *Domains* button in the *App Sidebar*. CloudCannon will open the *Domains browser*.
3. Identify the domain you want to update and click on the *Domain* card. CloudCannon will open the *Domain* page for this Custom Domain.
4. On the *DNS* tab, click the *\+ Add Record* button. CloudCannon will open the *Add DNS record* modal.
5. Select the type of DNS record you want to add using the *Type* dropdown. CloudCannnon will update the fields in the *Add DNS record* modal depending on the record type.
6. Enter the details for your DNS record in the appropriate fields.
7. Click the *Add Record* button.

CloudCannon will add the DNS record to your Custom Domain.

You can edit or delete existing DNS records from the *DNS* tab by selecting the *Edit* or *Delete* options from the *Context Menu* on the DNS record card.

<comp.Notice info_type="important">
  Deleting a DNS record will permanently remove the record details. If you need to enter these details into another DNS provider, please ensure you have a correct copy of your record details before you delete it.
</comp.Notice>
